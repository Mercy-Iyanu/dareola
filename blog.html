About Dare

As a seasoned IT professional with over a decade of experience, Dare Olayiwola is passionate about merging cutting-edge technology with real-world applications. With expertise in biometric systems, deep learning, and cybersecurity, Dare holds a Ph.D. in Pattern Recognition and has contributed significantly to biometric access control, language processing, and IoT security. Currently serving as a Senior Regional Director at Sabre Travel Network, he drives innovation across multiple African countries, ensuring business development, technical excellence, and customer satisfaction. His career is built on a foundation of problem-solving, teamwork, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
